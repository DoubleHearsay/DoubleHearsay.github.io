Will to Power

Don't mispronounce my name. It's Knee-cha in Friedrich Nieztche.

So apparently these guys have been telling you what's important? Nonsense!


  • Humans have freedom.
  • Humans also have ambitions and talents.
  • However, they are told to be guilty of their powerful stregnths.
  • Humans also have weaknesses..
  • However, they are told to accept that and be content.
  • Noble power is no longer valued in society as people constantly join the bandwagon.
  • The world is divided into good and evil to be easy to judge.
  • People don't face reality.
  • Humans go through a metamorphesis.
  • They are first a camel to withstand the ideas of the world.
  • They then must become a lion to fight off the old ideas.
  • They are then a child to recreate their own world.

It may be controversial, but it works nonetheless. Humans don't explore their true ambition and power because they are told that power is bad by society; they are then held back.

The important thing is, you do not have to obey any preordained life. It is up to you to choose your own purpose and escape the bandwagon.

What I'm talking about is my concept of the Will to Power. Individuals must go beyond good and evil to take on the whole of life. That is living like a superperson, or an Ubermensch. They realize that they are built up by the good things AND the bad things that happen to them.

Unfortunately, my works were used as Nazi propaganda after my death despite the fact that I harshly opposed mass movements.
