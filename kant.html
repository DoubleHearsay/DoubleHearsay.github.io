Utility Calculus

Oh no! Math! I'm Jeremy Bentham. But if you want help making the right choices in life, you've come to the right place.

Whenever you make a choice: simply measure these aspects of the choice and the pleasure that it should bring.


  • Intensity: How powerful is the pleasure caused? Is the experience really good?
  • Duration: How long will the pleasure caused by that action last?
  • Certainty: Are you sure your action will cause pleasure or not? You could be wrong.
  • Propinquity: How soon will the effect kick in?
  • Fecundity: Will that pleasure propagate other pleasure?
  • Purity: Will the pleasure be followed by pain?
  • Extent: How many people are being affected by this?

That's a lot of thinking. But in the end, this ends up with the greatest happiness, something no one can dispute. Who doesn't like pleasure?

What I'm talking about is my concept of the Ulility Calculus. Goodness can be calculated. Just you.
