Categorical Imperative

Nature walk, check! I'm Immanuel Kant.

People truly are overthinking the choice making process. Check this out:


  • When making a choice...
  • 1.Look at the ACTION
  • 2.Take that action, and make it so that everyone will repetedly do that.
  • 3.If there is a victim of the action, you are the victim.
  • 4.Now do something better.
  • 5.Ignore the personal consequences of the specific action.
  • 6.Revel in your moral integrity

Seems pretty easy, but such actions require the greatest moral integrity. We always give ourselves excuses for doing something. But how far can be give ourselves excuses.

What I'm talking about is my concept of the Categorical Imperative. We know no good can come from bad. Just you.
