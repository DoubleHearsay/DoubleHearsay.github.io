Stoicism

Don't expect much from me. I am Marcus Aurelius. The Literal Philosopher King.

Look at this logic.


  • We have high expectations of life.
  • Become angry and enraged when those points aren't reached.
  • We have to choose to change our product or our expectation.
  • Guess which one is easier to control?
  • It's not like you can control whether it rains or "weather" it doesn't.

It might seem pessimistic, but at least stoics are generally happy if not...stoic.

If someone bad happens, we aren't affected. If something ok happens, we're genuinly happy!.

We honestly expect too much from such an imperfect world. But we can prepare ourselves from this imperfect world.

What I'm talking about is Stoicism.

Remember this: Nothing happens to you that you aren't ready for. Regardless of all the struggles, you'll still be there. Everything's gonna be alright.
