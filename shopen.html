Will to Life

I'm not Wolverine! I'm Arthur Schopenhaur!!

Life kinda sucks. Not too much to make out of it:


  • Humans function like animals.
  • But humans are smart.
  • Therefore, they can contemplate their existence and be miserable.
  • Our primal instincts prevent us from living truly virtuous lives.
  • The only way to avoid this is to live an ascetic life with no desires.

Looks bleak. That's because it is. One example is of a natural attraction to other humans. They cultivate a relationship but when they produce a child, they are prone to domestic violence, disagreements, and general unahppiness.

It makes sesnse doens't it? Once we really get to know someone, we find out that they are horrible people. But it's too late, you already paid for child support.

What I'm talking about is my concept of the Will to Life. Truly, the only way to escape from this is to denounce desires and to reject your human nature. Just you.

If they say, "I love you" in the first six months, watch out! In fact, watch out even if they don't!
